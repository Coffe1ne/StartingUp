Filip Mateski - Autobiography

I was expected to drop-out, I’ve held myself to a higher standard, lost respect from my family,I tell you sincerely that I’m serious about changing my life,Once I’ve been criticized,Twice I’ve been shaped into the man I am now.

I bought things that further my passions,I love striving for greatness,I’ve been scared of failure,I’ve seen said failure first hand,I’m not giving up.

I learned this isn’t easy, I’ve heard it’s impossible,I’ve had set backs in my life,I once thought about giving up.And only one person can truly hold me accountable, that’s me I have been upset with some choices I’ve made I witnessed that with a little time you can work your way back from anything I will testify that you can literally do anything as long as you believe whole heartedly that you can.

I have stories about times that I’ve had no hope,I found hope in the midst of those stories.
